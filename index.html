WHAT YOU’RE COVERED FOR

The big stuff, the small stuff, and the unexpected stuff

Fire, Theft & Explosion

Provides financial protection if your food truck, equipment, or stock is damaged or lost due to fire, theft, or sudden explosions.

Malicious Damage

Protects your vehicle and setup if someone intentionally damages your truck, equipment or signage, keeping you safe from costly repairs.

Public & Products Liability

Protects you against claims if someone is injured or becomes ill due to your food, equipment, or business activities.

Collision or Overturning

Covers accidental damage when your vehicle hits something, is struck by another, or overturns during operation or movement.

Transit Damage

Protects your truck, fit-out, equipment and stock from accidental damage while being transported or moved between locations.

Equipment & Fit-Out Protection

Covers your fryers, grills, coffee machines and full fit-out against accidental damage, breakdown or loss, keeping your business running smoothly.
